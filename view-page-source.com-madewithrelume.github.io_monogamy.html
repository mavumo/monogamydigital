Webflow is a visual website builder, hosting platform and Content Management System used by leading tech companies around the world.

We run virtual workshops using a combination of design and video conferencing tools. We facilitate it just like we would in person, except we’re using a virtual whiteboard and post-it notes.

A wireframe represents the skeletal framework of a website. It's used early in the process to lay out content and functionality on a page before visual design and content is created.

High fidelity designs are visual representations of what the website will look like. We design each page of the website before we begin development so that we can iterate on the design at a faster pace and plan for the development phase more effectively.

A web design system is a collection of reusable components that can be assembled together to build any number of web pages. Combined with Webflow, a design system can empower you to build out web pages yourself, without the need of a developer.

How it works

Here’s an overview of what’s involved to create and publish your website.

1-2 hours

Briefing session

Figure out what to do...

Discuss your goals, determine success metrics, identify problems to solve and define visitors of the website. This can take place in-person or virtually.

1-2 weeks

UX design + content strategy

Begin designing...

Design wireframes for the entire website to map out key customer journeys and understand what content will be needed to communicate your product or service.

1-2 weeks

Visual design + branding

Make it sexy...

Define visual direction of the website and translate the wireframes into high-fidelity designs that successfully communicate your company’s brand and product or service.

2-4 weeks

Website development

Bring it to life...

Build out the high-fidelity designs into a custom website & web design system. We then integrate Webflow’s CMS to enable easy updates to the website.

1-2 hours

Client onboarding

Handing over site ownership...

Provide you with a 1-on-1 training session and a library of personalised tutorials so that you are well equipped to take control of your website.

Once your website is ready to go live, you will always be in full control

In 2021, we can all agree that there's absolutely no need to wait for weeks or fork out additional fees for developers to make the necessary updates to your web property.

That’s why we use world-class platforms like Webflow or Shopify to provide you with an easy-to-use dashboard and Content Management System made for marketing and design teams. With our support, you’ll get the hang of it in no time!
